These are some of the resources you get access to as a SWE National member.

Scholarships

The SWE Scholarship Program provides financial assistance for the education of SWE National Members in preparation for careers in engineering, engineering technology and computer science. Stay tuned for MIT SWE Scholarship applications opening up in the Fall!

Resume and Cover Letter Resources

A resume briefly describes a candidate's education, experience, and other relevant attributes for a potential employer. A cover letter is a document sent to employers with a resume to provide additional information. These two together are your chance to impress a company. Often the easiest way to craft a resume or cover letter is to start from samples and then fill in your own experiences.

Company Contacts

We have contacts in many companies that are looking to hire students in a wide range of majors and for a variety of job types. These companies have agreed to share their contact information specifically with MIT SWE National Members, and they are happy to talk with you and answer any questions you have. Please respect their privacy and do not share these contacts with anyone who is not an MIT SWE National Member. Check out the list to start reaching out!
