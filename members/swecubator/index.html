What is SWEcubator?


  • The MIT SWEcubator (SWE Incubator) program provides mentorship, resources, and funding to help SWE National Members start new initiatives aligned with the SWE mission.

  • Interested in applying? Fill out the form at the bottom of the page!

  • Questions? Contact the MIT SWE Tech Department.

Previous Initiatives


  • #HelloWorld Bootcamp in Des Plaines, IL (Haripriya M) - high school students mentored 30 middle school students and taught them how to code in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Guest speakers including university professors, STEM teachers and patent holders spoke about their work in STEM. The camp culminated in an event where the middle school students presented their final websites on a topic of social good to an audience of hundred people, including superintendents, principals, city officials, teachers and parents. Students received t-shirts and VR headsets for their amazing work.
