WHAT IS MIT SWE?

MIT Society of Engineers was founded in 1979. Since then, we have strived to inspire younger generations about engineering, encourage the notion of diversity in engineering, and determine and advocate for the needs of women engineers at MIT and the broader community. Our departments include:

  • Campus Relations
  • Career Development
  • Membership
  • Outreach
  • Technology
