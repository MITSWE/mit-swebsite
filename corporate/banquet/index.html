The Society of Women Engineers (SWE) Career Fair Banquet is a networking dinner on the evening of the career fair. The event will take place from 6:30 to 9 p.m. on Friday, September 29th, 2017 at no cost to students or employers. The event is open to students and employers of all genders. Business casual attire is required.

If you are a student and would like to attend the banquet, please apply here by Monday, September 18th at 11:59 p.m. ET.

Employer registration has closed.

If you have any questions, please contact swe-cr@mit.edu.
