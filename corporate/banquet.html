The annual SWE Career Fair Banquet is a networking dinner on the evening of the MIT Fall Career Fair. The event will take place from 6:30pm to 9pm at the Hyatt Regency Cambridge at no cost to students or employers. The event is open to all companies registered for the 2016 MIT Fall Career Fair.

If you are interested in attending, please register for the 2016 Fall Career Fair and fill out both the Company Information and Event Partnership forms below.
