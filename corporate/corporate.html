Fall Career Fair Banquet

MIT SWE is one of the three student groups which organize the MIT Fall Career Fair. Over 3500 MIT students and 500 companies attend the fair. Each year, SWE hosts a banquet attended by select SWE members and employers. We encourage you to sign up to attend the fair and banquet!
