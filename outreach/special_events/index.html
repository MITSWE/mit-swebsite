Cambridge Science Festival

Dates : Apr 19 | 11am - 2pm | MIT Museum


Mission to Mars with MIT SWE

It is the year 2050, and you have been selected to join an elite team of engineers to design rovers for discovering water resources on Mars. This is a critical mission in order for humans to live on Mars! Join us for a 3 hour workshop to learn the design process to create your own Mars rover, using simple machines and Newton's laws of motion! Are you up for the mission?

Cost : $10 per participant (includes Museum adm>ission) Ages 9-12. Preregistration required at web.mit.edu/museum/programs/festival.html.

Questions? Contact the MIT SWE Festival Chairs.
