Brownie Girl Scouts & Junior Girl Scouts Outreach

What is Girl Scouts Outreach?

The Girl Scouts outreach committee hosts two series of events for Brownie Girl Scouts and Junior Girl Scouts. Three morning-long sessions will be held each semester, with each session consisting of fun hands-on experiments such as extracting DNA from strawberries and straw bridge building. Brownie Girl Scouts Day is held in the fall semester, and Junior Girl Scout Day is held in the spring semester.


Brownie Girl Scout Days

Dates : Fall Only

Brownie Girl Scout Days is a three session program which will take place in Fall 2019 for Brownie Girl Scouts in the Boston area. Each hands-on, interactive session focuses on learning about a different topic with your fellow Brownie Scouts. These events will consist of science and engineering based activities, related to the great planet we live on, chemistry in cooking and much more! Feel free to join us for one, two, or all three sessions. Brownie Girl Scout Days will take place on MIT's campus. These events will take place from 10 AM - 2 PM, and will include lunch and snacks. We can't wait to see you all there!


Junior Girl Scouts

Dates : April 6, May 4, May 11 | 10am-2pm

Junior Girl Scout Days is a three session program which will take place in Spring 2019 for Junior Girl Scouts in the Boston area. Each hands-on, interactive session focuses on learning about a different topic with your fellow Junior Girl Scouts. These events will consist of science and engineering based activities, related to math, biology, and space! Feel free to join us for one, two, or all three sessions.

Sign up for our first event, Bounce, Stretch, Float here to explore material science with us!

Questions? Contact the MIT SWE Girl Scout Chairs.
