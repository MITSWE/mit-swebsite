High School Outreach - Beaverdash!

What is the BeaverDash?

Come join us for BeaverDash on Saturday, April 20th, 2019 from 10am-3pm! BeaverDash is a fast-paced, high-energy design and building challenge for all high school students interested in STEM. This annual design and engineering competition challenges teams of 3-4 students to think quickly and creatively to solve real world problems. BeaverDash is an opportunity for local high school students to learn about practical engineering techniques while practicing innovative thinking. An MIT undergraduate student is assigned to each team to mentor and guide the teams while raising awareness of engineering fields and disciplines.
