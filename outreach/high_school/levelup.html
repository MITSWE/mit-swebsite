High School Outreach - Level Up!

About

LevelUP-Pathways in STEM is MIT SWE's premier Mini-Conference/(Symposium) for High Schoolers. The goal of LevelUP is to introduce High School students to new and interesting or unexpected pathways in STEM. Through a series of Tech Talk presenters, ranging from undergraduate students to professors to industry professionals, students will learn about a variety of interesting opportunities and careers available in STEM. Over lunch, students will have the opportunity to interact with the morning's speakers as well as MIT SWE members. The second half of LevelUP will consist of hands-on workshops so participants can engage their mind and their hands.
