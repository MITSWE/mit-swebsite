High School Outreach - Exploring Majors Fair

Check back in fall to sign up for the Exploring Majors Fair!

The Exploring Majors Fair is an opportunity for high school students to meet MIT students and ask about majors, research, college and more! This is a great chance for students to gain insight into the lives of college engineering students, to learn what opportunities are available in STEM fields, and have the opportunity to ask questions concerning STEM. Please contact the MIT SWE High School Outreach Chairs if you have any questions.
