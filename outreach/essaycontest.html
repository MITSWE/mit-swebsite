High School Outreach - Essay Contest

MIT SWE is hosting its annual annual science and engineering essay competition! Our mission is to promote interest in the STEM fields and encourage high school students to explore its implications on changing the world.

Cash prizes will be awarded! There will be a 1st place, 2nd place, and 3rd place winner as well as 3 honorable mentions.

Winners will be emailed individually.


The 2015 essay prompt is:

"What are ways to inspire younger generations to pursue STEM? Be creative and please focus your answer on inspiration not just raising awareness."

The deadline to submit an essay is extended to October 16, 11:59pm.
