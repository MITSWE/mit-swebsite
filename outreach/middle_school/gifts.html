Off-Campus Outreach - GIFTS

Dates : 2/25, 3/3, 3/10, 3/17, 3/24, 3/31 |Browne Middle School in Chelsea, MA

GIFTS is an after-school program for middle school students at Browne Middle School in Chelsea, MA. Our goal is to give students the opportunity to gain exposure to science that is outside of the normal curriculum. Every week, we do science activities that are educational, eye-opening, and fun!

Questions? Contact the MIT SWE Off Campus Education Chairs.
