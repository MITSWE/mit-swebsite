KEYs - Keys to Empowering Youth

KEYs Spring 2020 Activity

Top Secret Spy: Online!

If you're looking for something fun to do, check out these cryptology and finger printing activities! Learn how to encode, decode, and crack the Caesar cipher and more here. If you have any questions or comments, please email swe-keys@mit.edu!


KEYs Spring 2019 Sessions

Dates : Apr. 6, Apr. 27, May 18 (all Saturdays) | 10 a.m. - 3 p.m. | MIT campus

KEYs is an outreach program for middle school girls that introduces them to various topics in science and math via mostly hands-on activities. In the past, we've done activities such as building a model rocket and using the science of acid-base chemistry to write messages in "invisible ink".

All sessions are free! It is on a first come, first serve basis. Sign up here.

If you have any questions at all, don't hesitate to send an email to our SWE Keys Coordinators.
